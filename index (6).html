Timeline of Scientific Discoveries

Ancient Discoveries Modern Discoveries Recent Discoveries

Ancient Discoveries

  • 3500 BC

    Wheel - Invented in Mesopotamia, transforming transportation and engineering.

  • Prehistoric

    Fire - Early humans discovered how to control fire, leading to cooking and warmth.

  • 500 BC

    Pythagoras' Theorem - Ancient Greeks discovered fundamental principles in mathematics.

  • 1543

    Heliocentric Theory - Nicolaus Copernicus proposed that the Earth revolves around the Sun.

Modern Discoveries (16th to 20th Century)

  • 1687

    Gravity - Isaac Newton formulated the law of universal gravitation.

  • 18th Century

    Electricity - Benjamin Franklin and others made foundational discoveries about electricity.

  • 1830

    Darwins Finches - Charles Darwin observed these finches during his voyage on the HMS Beagle in the 1830s.

  • 1930

    Geological Timescale - Holmes discovered First absolute geological timescale .

  • 1953

    DNA Structure - Watson, Crick, and Franklin discovered the double helix structure of DNA.

Recent Discoveries (21st Century)

  • 2012

    Higgs Boson - Discovered at CERN, explaining why particles have mass.

  • 2015

    Gravitational Waves - Detected by LIGO, confirming Einstein's theory of relativity.

  • 2012

    CRISPR Gene Editing - A tool to edit genes with unprecedented precision.

  • 2019

    First Image of a Black Hole - Captured by the Event Horizon Telescope.
